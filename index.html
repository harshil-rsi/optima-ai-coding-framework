Component 2: KTS Plan for Tailored Transition

A proven 3-phase plan to reach steady-state maintenance with confidence

1

Knowledge Gathering

  • Deep-dive using Knowledge Assistant
  • Shadow SMEs and on-call rotations
  • Inventory critical flows & SLAs
  • Capture ticket cadences
  • Map integrations & dependencies
Deliverables:

Current-state docs, architecture brief, runbook backlog, risk register

2

Transition

  • Execute P2/P3 tickets independently
  • Co-pilot on P1 incidents
  • Build P0 simulation drills
  • Author runbooks & training
  • Harden observability
Deliverables:

Runbooks, training assets, prompt library, readiness report

3

Steady State

  • Independent delivery across P0-P3
  • Optimize flow with agents
  • Continuous improvement
  • SLO reviews & post-mortems
  • Capability upgrades
Deliverables:

Quarterly improvement plan, governance dashboard, automation backlog

Component 4: Certification Ladder (Enablement)

Goal: Motivate and standardize proficiency for maintainers

🥉 Level 1 – Foundation

  • Completes onboarding trail
  • Passes codebase map quiz
  • Resolves guided P3 tickets

🥈 Level 2 – Specialist

  • Independently resolves P2/P3
  • Authors/updates runbooks
  • Participates in P1 drills

🥇 Level 3 – Product Owner

  • Leads P0/P1 incidents
  • Designs automation
  • Mentors peers
  • Steers quarterly improvements
